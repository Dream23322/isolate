About Us

Isolate Anticheat is based off of Scythe anticheat, one of the biggest Minecraft Bedrock anticheats. I noticed the owner has began putting less effort in and also that the anticheat has some major flaws. Isolate was made to fix these, giving a more professional feel, and being target to a specific server type.

The Anticheat is in Beta! If you find any false flags please report them to 4urxra#0 on discord!

Inspiration

People or projects that have inspired the creation of Isolate. 

Alice Anticheat

Alice Anticheat was a Minecraft Java edition anticheat, originally made by NikV2. It was by far the best anticheat for Minecraft Java Edition. I got some of the ideas from it, such as the way one of the fly checks work and how the scaffold checks detect.
